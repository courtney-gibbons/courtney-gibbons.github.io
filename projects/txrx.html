Wireless Transceiver

I was on the hardware team to design a wireless transmitter and receiver at 27 MHz. I designed each component to meet design specifications and simulated them in LT Spice. My Colpitt's Oscillators performed the best on the team, so I designed and soldered the 22 MHz and 4 MHz Oscillator PCBs, then we assembled and tested as a team.
