About me

Hi, my name is Courtney Gibbons! I am in my third year studying electrical engineering at UCLA with a computer science technical breadth in the Fast Track to Success program. This program selects 30 students to take honors classes and do research. I currently research in the Quantum Biology Tech (QuBiT) lab, and I intern in the summers as a software engineer at Microsoft. Outside of school and work, I love playing soccer, hiking, painting, and doing personal projects!
