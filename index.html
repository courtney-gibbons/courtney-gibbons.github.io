About me

Hi, my name is Courtney Gibbons! I am going into my third year studying electrical engineering at UCLA with a computer science technical breadth in the Fast Track to Success program. This program selects 30 students to take honors classes and do research. I currently research in the Quantum Biology Tech (QuBiT) lab, and I am interning as a software engineer at Microsoft. Outside of school and work, I love playing soccer (go Liverpool!), hiking, painting, and doing personal projects!

Club Project

Facial Recognition in Python

I coded a headless Raspberry Pi and camera module in Python to 1) detect and save images of a user's face to a directory with their name, 2) train a machine learning model on the images of the desired user's face versus the faces of other users, and 3) use the machine learning model to recognize the desired user's face among other faces.
